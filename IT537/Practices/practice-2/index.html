Omer's Absolutely Ridiculous Space Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup invisible water
  • 2 spoons of dramatic silence
  • 3 clouds (finely chopped)
  • 1 confused tomato
  • 3 cup emotional flour
  • 1 pinch of keyboard dust

For the filling:

  • 1 cup totally fake sand (edible imagination version only)
  • 2 slices of wireless cheese
  • 1 flying cucumber (grounded before use)
  • 4 drops of panic sauce

Instructions

  1. First, put the invisible water into a very real bowl and stare at it until it feels uncomfortable.
  2. Add the dramatic silence and mix it slowly with a wooden spoon that clearly does not understand its job.
  3. Gently add the imaginary sand to the bowl and promise everyone that it is totally safe and not real sand.
  4. Now take a glass bowl and carefully pretend to pour it out of a window in your imagination only.
  5. Make absolutely sure that nobody is actually there, because this is a peaceful, fictional cooking show.
  6. Take the now empty bowl and congratulate it for surviving such an intense emotional journey.
  7. Add the wireless cheese and panic sauce, then whisk aggressively until the mixture starts judging you.
  8. Roll the dough into a very questionable shape that looks nothing like food.
  9. Serve it proudly on an empty plate and tell everyone it is a "conceptual sandwich".
  10. Do not eat it. Just upload a photo and call it modern cuisine.
